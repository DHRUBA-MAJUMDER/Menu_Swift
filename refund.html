Refund Policy

Transparency and trust — our top priority for every customer.

Last Updated: 12 September 2025

1. Overview

At MenuSwift, we aim to deliver the best digital restaurant solutions. If you are not satisfied with our service, you may be eligible for a refund under certain conditions.

2. Eligibility for Refund

  • Refund requests must be made within 30 days of purchase.
  • Only first-time subscription payments are eligible for refunds.
  • No refund will be provided for renewals or plan upgrades.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Services already delivered or used.
  • Technical issues caused by user’s own network or devices.
  • Violation of our Terms & Conditions.

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact our support team with your transaction ID and registered email. Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

5. Contact Us

For refund-related queries, contact our support team at support@menuswift.in.
