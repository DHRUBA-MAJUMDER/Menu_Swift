Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Last Updated: 12 September 2025

1. Introduction

At MenuSwift, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number)
  • Restaurant-related data (menu items, orders, sales records)
  • Device and usage information for performance analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our restaurant management platform
  • To communicate with you about updates and support
  • To ensure security and prevent fraudulent activities

4. Data Security

We implement strict security measures using encrypted databases (Firebase) and secure authentication systems to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact our support team for any data-related requests.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you about significant changes through our platform or via email.

7. Contact Us

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@menuswift.in.
