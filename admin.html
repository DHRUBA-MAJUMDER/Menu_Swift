Building a Contactless Future for Restaurants

As a BCA graduate from Assam, I identified a critical gap in the restaurant industry — the ordering process was slow, error-prone, and unhygienic. My startup provides a complete digital contactless ordering system that transforms how restaurants operate.

From QR code scanning to real-time order tracking, every aspect is designed for speed, accuracy, and delightful user experience.

Zero contact ordering via QR codes
Real-time kitchen-to-table communication
Integrated payment gateway (UPI, Cards)
Smart analytics for restaurant owners
Multi-language support for diverse India

Process

How It Works

Four simple steps from scanning to savoring.

1

Scan the QR Code

Each table has a unique QR code. Customers scan it using any phone camera — no app required.

2

Browse & Customize

View the full menu with photos, descriptions, and prices. Add items, customize spice levels, and more.

3

Place & Track Order

Confirm your order and watch it move from kitchen preparation to ready-to-serve in real time.

4

Pay Digitally

When done, pay using UPI, card, or wallet. Get a digital receipt instantly on your phone.
